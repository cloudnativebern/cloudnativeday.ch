Sustainability

Our goal is to make Swiss Cloud Native Day more ecologically sustainable while continuing to provide an amazing event. Our concept for achieving this consists of multiple categories and measures. Some of them we already implement, some of them are plans for the upcoming events.

Venue and Logistics

Encourage the use of public transportation by providing detailed information on bus and train routes to the venue. Donâ€™t invite too many speakers who come from afar and have to travel by air.

Reducing Waste

Minimize paper usage by providing digital copies of schedules, maps, and presentation materials through the conference website. Implement a zero-waste policy by providing reusable or compostable serving ware for meals and snacks. Set up clearly labeled recycling and compost bins throughout the venue.

Food and Catering

Make sure the caterer uses locally sourced, organic, and seasonal ingredients. Offer vegetarian and vegan meal options and promote them as such. Minimize food waste by accurately estimating attendee numbers and coordinating with caterers to adjust servings accordingly. Contact Foodsharing to pick up leftovers.

Energy Efficiency

Utilize energy-efficient equipment throughout the event.

Promoting Eco-Friendly Practices

Raise awareness among participants about the importance of sustainability through pre-conference communication, signage, and presentations. Have sustainability-focused talks.

Offsetting Carbon Footprint

Donate profits to sustainability organizations.

Measurement and Continuous Improvement

Collect data on travel, energy usage, waste generation, and carbon emissions before, during, and after the event to measure its ecological impact. Use participant feedback to identify areas for improvement and implement changes in future conferences. Share our sustainability initiatives and results with other conference organizers to inspire broader adoption of eco-friendly practices in the industry. Share the initiatives and results with participants to create transparency.

By implementing these strategies, we aim to be an ecologically sustainable event while creating a positive and memorable experience for all participants.
